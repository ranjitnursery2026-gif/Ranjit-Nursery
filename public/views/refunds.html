Returns & Refunds

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Plant Replacements

Because plants are living entities, we do not accept general returns. However, if your plant arrives severely damaged or dead, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with clear photographs. We will arrange a replacement or store credit at our discretion.

2. Non-Plant Items

For pots, tools, and accessories, you may return the items within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unused, in their original packaging, and in resalable condition.

3. Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed and credited to your original method of payment within 5-7 working days. For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds will be issued via UPI or Bank Transfer.

4. Cancellations

You may cancel your order before it has been dispatched for a full refund. Once the order has been handed over for delivery, it cannot be canceled.
